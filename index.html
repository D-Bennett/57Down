About

Hi,

We’re 57 Down.

We are a very original Four piece: Two bass players, one drummer, and one vocalist. No guitarist (or cheesewire monkeys, as we like to call them).

We have no set style or agenda!

We’re a tight unit, with a melodic, heavy, punky sound (which is hauntingly beautiful on occasion).

Our influences are difficult to pin down as we are treading a new path, but, these bass players are our main influences:

  • John Entwistle
  • Geezer Butler
  • Peter Hook
  • Chris Wolstenholme
  • Morgan Nichols

How did we get our name?

Well, it's simple.

A massive blackhole has been discovered that emits the lowest note in the known universe, 57 octaves below middle C.

Hence, two bass players, low groovy tunes — 57 Down.

Moreover, we're signed to the fantastic Blackened Death Records; a label that "gets" us, and, most importantly, they're fans of our music.

Until the next time,

Keep rockin!
